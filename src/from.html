How to plan the trip

We suggest you to book in advance accommodation and conference ticket. In this way you can get good deals.

In Italy we have (as in the rest of Eurpoe) 220V and 50Mh; we suggest you to buy a plug adapter

The best tips?

Bring with you:

  • your laptop/tablet/smartphone
  • euros and/or credit/debit card to withdraw
  • your business cards
  • your digital camera, Turin is a fantastic city

Do before:

  • check your passport or ID
  • don't eat pizza, coffee or wine before: we have the best ones

How much I'll spend?

Turin is a cheap city, here any examples:

  • espresso: 1 euro
  • breakfast in a bar: 3-4 euro
  • beer/coke in a bar/pub: 4 euros
  • sandwich: 3 euros
  • dinner in a pizzeria: 15-20 euros
  • bottle of wine (take away): 5 euro
  • subway ticket: 1.5 euro
  • 4* hotel room: 50-70 euros

Any good hotel?

We suggest to stay in the city center, because the hotels are cheap and you can go to the conference by walk or with bus/subway.

Hotels usually include "city tax". When you book an hotel, take in mind that is breakfast is not included you can have one in a bar for 3-4 euros (espresso, croissant, sandwhich).

If you can, book one of theese hotels: they are cheap, clean and close to the conference (max 10 minutes by walk) and to bus shuttle: Dock Milano, Gran Mogol, Abanyta, Urbani, Luxor.

Or, if you prefer, check these searches for 1 people for 1, 2 or 3 nights on Booking.com.

Best Flights and connections?

The main airport of Turin, "Torino Caselle", is 40 minutes away from the city center (with the bus, that runs every 30/60 minutes). The airport is small and offers quick check in and security controls.
